Explore Nevada City

While you're in town

Nestled in the Sierra Nevada foothills, Nevada City is a charming Gold Rush-era town full of history, natural beauty, and delightful discoveries. Here are some of our favorite ways to experience it.

Historic Downtown

Wander through Victorian-era streets lined with boutique shops, art galleries, and cozy cafes.

Wine Tasting

Explore local wineries and tasting rooms showcasing Sierra Foothills vintages.

Hiking & Nature

Discover beautiful trails at South Yuba River State Park or the nearby Tahoe National Forest.

Farm-to-Fork Dining

Savor the local culinary scene with restaurants featuring fresh, locally-sourced ingredients.

Gold Rush History

Visit historic sites and museums that tell the story of California's Gold Rush era.

Local Breweries

Sample craft beers at local breweries with mountain-town atmosphere.
